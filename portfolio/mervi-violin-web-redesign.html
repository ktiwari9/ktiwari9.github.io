Complete website overhaul

Mervi Myllyoja is a violinist performing and teaching in Helsinki, Finland. She is known for her versatile crossover style and has collaborated with top rated artists around the world. When she came to me, she had an outdated website that needed a complete makeover. You can see her old website below.



After thorough brain-storming we concluded that this website was no longer in line with her current career goals and needed a facelift for better user experience. This, we settled for a hybrid website with a single page portfolio but also shows the event calendar so people can attend her gigs.





Checkout her live website and you are welcome to contact me if you would like one too.
