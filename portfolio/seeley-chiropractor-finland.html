Revamped Instagram Bio

When I started working with Dr. Jack Seeley, Chiropractor based in Finland, I noticed that the bio did not bring out his USP and a clear call-to-action for the potential clients. I also noticed that some of the business essential highlights were missing. So, I worked on optimizing the bio, and the other facets of the business page such as accurate business category. I also helped curate customized hashtag sets to increase the reach and prepared a content strategy to help Dr. Jack reach the ideal audience.
